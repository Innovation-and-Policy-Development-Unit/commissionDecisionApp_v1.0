  ✔ Required documents checklist per submission type
  ✔ Workflow timeline — full history of all stage transitions with actor and remarks

  ✔ Auto-cascade of Commission decisions — when a Form 2-1 is Approved/Rejected, all attached Form 2-2s automatically receive the same decision
